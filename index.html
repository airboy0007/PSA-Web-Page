Our Visionary Mission

Located at Chongi Stop, Swabi, Parwaaz e Shaheen Academy has been a beacon of knowledge for years. Our mission is to provide an environment where every student can achieve their full potential.

We are proud to announce our recent strategic merger with Kanz-ul-Iman Beacon School. This partnership allows us to offer a seamless educational experience from morning school sessions to specialized afternoon tuition.
